Jolly Phonics Lessons App

Over 2M users worldwide

It is an app for teachers that contains interactive content to help them teach a phonics lesson.

A Jolly Phonics lesson has 8 key steps, the App takes the teacher through the delivery of each step.

It also contains fun games for pupils, that test pupil’s sound knowledge, and adapts to their ability.

The App is designed for the developing world. It requires a small amount of space and functions completely offline.

Jolly Monitor

The Jolly Monitoring system is a technology system designed to improve Jolly Phonics projects in developing countries.

The core principle is that we should understand how teachers are teaching, and how pupils are performing, whereever they are. Once project implementers understanding the teaching, and pupil performance, they can intervene to improve outcomes.

Reach for the stars,
African readers app

Reach for the Stars provides fun and interactive reading books, with stories contextually appropriate for African children.

The app allows users to tap on words that they don’t understand, to have the app say the sounds and blend them together.

The app is continuously updated with more books, and exciting stories for children to practice their reading.
